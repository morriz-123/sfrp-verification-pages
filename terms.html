2. About the Service

The application is called Verification for SFRP and is operated by the San Francisco Roleplay development and ownership team.

The service is used to connect a Roblox account to a Discord member inside the San Francisco Roleplay community. After successful verification, the service may:

  • Confirm the Roblox account connected to the user.
  • Record the Roblox user ID, username, and verification date.
  • Remove an unverified Discord role.
  • Assign the appropriate verified Discord roles.
  • Update the member's Discord nickname to match their Roblox username when the bot has permission to do so.
  • Help prevent duplicate or conflicting account verification.

3. Roblox OAuth Authorization

Verification is completed through Roblox's official OAuth authorization system. The application requests only the following OAuth scopes:

  • openid — used to identify the authorized Roblox account.
  • profile — used to obtain basic Roblox profile information needed for verification.

Depending on the information Roblox makes available, this may include your Roblox user ID, username, display name, and public profile information. The service may also retrieve your public Roblox avatar or headshot through Roblox's public thumbnail service.

We do not request access to create, edit, purchase, sell, trade, or manage Roblox items, Robux, groups, experiences, messages, friends, or account security settings.
